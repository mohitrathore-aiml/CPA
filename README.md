# Complaint Prioritization AI (CPA)

## Overview
Complaint Prioritization AI (CPA) is a decision-support system designed to help authorities efficiently analyze, categorize, and prioritize public complaints based on urgency,severity,location and public impact. The system focuses on **operational prioritization, automated decision-making**, ensures faster resolution and transparent governance.

---

## Key Objectives
### Benefits to Authority
- Reduce manual workload in complaint handling
- Assist authorities in identifying high-priority complaints
- Provide structured, explainable prioritization logic

### Benefits to Citizens
- Immediate handling of emergencies(accident, fallen poles, water leaks)
- predicts future problems hotspots and maintenance needs
- ensuring faster,fair and transparent resolutions
